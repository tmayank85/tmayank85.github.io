Step-by-Step Guide: Kaise Kare?

Chaar aasan steps mein apna professional email setup complete karein.

Sabse pehle, ek domain name khareedo jo aapke business se match karta ho. Godaddy ya Namecheap jaise platforms par yeh bahut saste mil jaate hain, lagbhag Rs. 500 per saal mein.

Apne domain ke DNS settings mein jaakar SPF aur DKIM records add karo. Yeh thoda technical lag sakta hai, lekin aapke SMTP service provider (jaise Mailgun) iske liye poori guide dete hain. Yeh one-time setup hai.

Naye domain se ekdum se hazaron emails na bhejein. Dheere-dheere quantity badhayein. Ek sample plan:

  • Week 1: 50 emails/day
  • Week 2: 100 emails/day
  • Week 3: 200 emails/day
  • Week 4: 500 emails/day

Bhejne se pehle, apni list ko Zerobounce ya Hunter.io jaise tool se verify karein. Kabhi bhi `info@`, `admin@` jaise role-based addresses par cold email na karein. Isse aapki bounce rate kam hogi aur reputation acchi rahegi.

Apne email campaigns ki performance track karna bahut zaroori hai. Dekho kitne emails open hue, kitne click hue. Mailchimp ya SendGrid jaise tools aapko detailed analytics dete hain. Isse aap apni strategy ko improve kar sakte ho.
